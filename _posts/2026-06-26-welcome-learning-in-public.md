This is the start of my learning log. Alongside the Hack The Box write-ups, I'm going to post short notes — daily or weekly — on whatever I'm digging into: a new technique, a tool that clicked, a box that humbled me, or a rabbit hole worth sharing.

The idea is simple: **learn out loud**. Writing something down forces me to actually understand it, and if it helps one other person, even better.

### What to expect

- **TIL notes** — small, specific things I learned that day.
- **Box reflections** — the thought process behind a solve, not just the steps.
- **Tooling** — scripts and setups that save me time.

> Game is sold, not told — unless it's about a hack. That knowledge is too juicy to withhold.

More soon. To publish a new note, I just drop a `YYYY-MM-DD-title.md` file in `_posts/`.
